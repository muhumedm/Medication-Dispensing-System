Privacy Policy

Purpose of the Website

The purpose of this website is to assist carers in tracking patients’ medication intake. It helps monitor missed medications, upcoming doses, and whether medications are taken on time or late.

Patient Contact Information

We collect the following patient contact information as part of your health record to ensure timely medication reminders and tracking. This information is used for the following purposes:

  • Email Address: Used to contact the assigned carer for notifications regarding medication, dosage, intake times, and related updates.
  • Date of Birth: Used to identify the patient's age for providing age-appropriate medication reminders and monitoring.
  • Age: Helps identify the patient's age for accurate medication tracking and reminders.
  • Sex/Gender: Used to ensure that the patient receives appropriate care and medications according to their gender.
  • Patient Number: Used to uniquely identify the patient in the system, ensuring correct medication tracking and updates.
  • Mobile Phone Number: Used to send notifications regarding medication reminders, and any necessary updates.

How We Use Your Data

We process the collected data to provide medication tracking, send timely reminders for medication adherence, and ensure all necessary health information is stored accurately for the assigned carer’s reference. The data is also used to:

  • Improve the system’s user experience and functionality.
  • Maintain security measures and resolve any technical issues.
  • Keep all patient and medication information secure.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law, to provide necessary services, or for user safety reasons (e.g., in case of medical emergencies).

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access, modify, or delete your personal data.
  • Request clarification on how your data is being processed.
  • Withdraw consent for processing your data where applicable.

If you have any concerns or need more details, you can contact our support team.
